Sutton Bar Room is a vintage themed neighborhood bar located in the cozy neighborhood of Sutton Place. It is known for its friendly and welcoming staff, excellent craft cocktails, extensive beer selection, and boisterous Monday Trivia Night. Whether you are a long-term resident of the neighborhood or just passing through, we are excited to host you!
